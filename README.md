# 🛰️ Control Systems Fundamentals: The Christopher Lum Archive

Welcome! This repository is a meticulously organized collection of resources for mastering **Control Systems**. Whether you're trying to wrap your head around frequency response or diving deep into state-space regulation, this archive bridges the gap between theoretical math and practical engineering.



> **Note:** These materials are based on the technical notes and instructional series by **Christopher Lum**, curated during the 2nd Year Computer Engineering curriculum at Alexandria University.

---

## 🚀 What's Inside?

This isn't just a folder of PDFs; it's a full toolkit for control engineering.

### 📚 The Lecture Vault (PDFs)
35+ detailed guides covering the entire spectrum of Classical and Modern Control:
* **The Foundations:** Transfer Functions, Block Diagram Algebra, and Time Response.
* **Stability & Performance:** Routh-Hurwitz, Steady State Errors, and 2nd Order Dynamics.
* **Frequency Domain:** Master-level guides on **Bode Plots** and **Root Locus**.
* **Advanced Control:** Full State Feedback (FSFB), **LQR** (Linear Quadratic Regulator), and Controllability.

### 💻 The Simulation Lab (`other_data/`)
Ready-to-run files to see the math in action:
* **MATLAB (`.m`):** Scripts for automated plotting, Ziegler-Nichols tuning, and system linearization.
* **Simulink (`.slx` / `.mdl`):** Visual models for **PID Control**, Anti-windup logic, and Flight Dynamics (Aerosonde & RCAM).
* **Mathematica (`.nb`):** Symbolic notebooks for deriving complex performance metrics.
